Carpooling Platform in the Great Toronto Areas

Way2Share Application is motivated by environmental crisis due to huge amount of car emissions. Transportation become one of the largest source of carbon dioxide emissions by direct fuel use. The amount of greenhouse and poisonous gases released into the atmosphere endangers the environment and causes severe health effects in humans. Carpooling is a effective way for GHG emission reductions. In addition, carpooling is economical personally and socially. Carpooling allows you to share the cost of gas and parking, cutting your expenses by nearly 50% or more and reduce the costs towards the construction of new roads, road maintenance and air pollution related health costs. This application provides online query of detailed information of your route, and how much you will save if you choose carpooling over driving alone. At our platform, you can find available carpooling around you and share your route with others. The application is aimed at contributing to improving environment and social economic by promoting carpooling.
